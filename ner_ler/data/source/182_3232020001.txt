Plant: Diablo Canyon 2.
Unit 2 Shutdown Required by Technical Specifications.
 On February 13, 2020, at 1025 hours Pacific Standard Time, during the performance of the quarterly control rod exercise surveillance test, Shutdown Bank "B" Group 1 became misaligned greater than 12 steps from its group demand position. In accordance with Technical Specification 3.1.4, "Rod Group Alignment Limits," Action D, a Unit 2 shutdown to Mode 3 was commenced at 1233 hours. The shutdown to Mode 3 was completed February 13, 2020, at 1545 hours. 

 This event is being reported per 10 CFR 50.73(a)(2)(i)(A) as the completion of any nuclear plant shutdown required by the plant's Technical Specifications. 

 This event was a result of rod misalignment caused by a failure of a slave cycler decoder lift card to provide proper timing signals.

 There was no impact on the health and safety of the public or plant personnel.
